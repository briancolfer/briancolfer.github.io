## Release Engineering and Delivery Consulting

I help teams improve how software moves from commit to production.

My focus is release engineering, CI/CD systems, deployment workflows, and delivery improvement. I am particularly interested in making software delivery more reliable, more repeatable, and less stressful for engineering teams.

Most teams do not have a tooling problem — they have a process, visibility, or decision problem. I work with teams to understand their current delivery system, identify risks and bottlenecks, and propose practical improvements.

## Areas I Help With

- CI/CD pipeline design and improvement
- Release and branching strategy
- Deployment automation
- Release checklists and runbooks
- Rollback and recovery strategy
- Pipeline reliability and build time reduction
- Delivery metrics (including DORA metrics)
- Observability for build and deployment systems
- Environment promotion strategy
- Decision models for evaluating delivery systems

## Typical Engagement

A typical engagement might include:

1. Review CI/CD pipeline and deployment workflow
2. Review release and branching strategy
3. Identify risks and bottlenecks
4. Suggest improvements
5. Help implement automation or process changes
6. Define delivery metrics
7. Document the release process

## Delivery Philosophy

The goal is not simply faster deployments.  
The goal is **reliable, repeatable delivery with clear feedback and low stress for the team**.

Good delivery systems allow teams to:
- Release frequently
- Recover quickly from failures
- Understand the state of their system
- Make better decisions over time
