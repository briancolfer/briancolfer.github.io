## Platform Engineering

[Conscious Platform Engineering](conscious-platform-engineering/)
A consultancy doctrine for deciding what to automate, what to surface, and what to escalate — protecting developer attention as carefully as infrastructure. Introduces a four-stage consulting framework and a set of paired measures for evaluating whether a platform is reducing meaningful risk or accumulating overhead.

## Continuous Delivery

[From Pipeline Gates to Decision Systems: Applying LSP to DevOps](lsp-cicd/)
Binary pass/fail pipeline gates force nuanced engineering decisions into a yes/no structure that doesn't reflect how engineers actually think. This article applies Logic Scoring of Preference to build a graded decision model on top of DORA metrics — enabling risk-aware deployment decisions rather than rigid boolean gates.
